Our Services

CONSTRUCTIONS

MOMENTOUS has been helping customers, both families and corporates, achieve their dream realty space.MOMENTOUS builders has successfully delivered more than 150 residential and commercial projects in India, while other 50+ projects are under development. Our projects include hi-tech townships, plots, villas, high-rise apartments, low-rise independent floors and commercial properties for office space and retail shops.

POWER DEVELOPMENT

MOMENTOUS's Power Development is equipped to take up both Thermal and Hydro Power Projects of all types.It is investing, operating and maintaining grid linked Independent Power Plants, Cogeneration and Captive Power Plants on Build-Own-Operate (BOO), Build-Own-Operate-Maintain (BOOM) and Build-Lease-Operate (BLO) basis

MATERIAL HANDLING

MOMENTOUS’s Minerals and Metals Business (M&M) offers one-stop solution for the ferrous and non-ferrous sectors. The ferrous business unit provides comprehensive solutions for the iron and steel industry while the non-ferrous business unit offers a spectrum of solutions for aluminium, copper, zinc, lead and mineral beneficiation plants.

MINING AND MACHINERY
